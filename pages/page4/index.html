Archons of Genshin World

The regions of Teyvat in Genshin Impact are governed by powerful beings known as Archons. Also known as The Seven, these gods are assigned one of the seven elements and preside over their part of Teyvat. Unlike other characters, the Archons have no need of Visions to control their element. Instead, they possess Gnoses, special items that are directly connected to Celestia, from which it’s believed the Archons draw their power. In the events of Genshin Impact, each of the Archons roam the world, disguised by their human persona. Some did not survive to the present day and have since been replaced by a “new” Archon. Others, however, have persisted until the Traveler’s appearance in Teyvat
