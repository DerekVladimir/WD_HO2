Static Cards

Mondstadt

The story goes: Mondstadt was ruled by the Aristocrats in the distant past, who periodically sacrificed slaves to a drake as tributes for a false treaty of peace. Vennessa, one of the slaves at the time, defeated the drake with the help of the Anemo Archon themselves, who had taken on the guise of a bard.

Liyue

What happens in the Liyue story? The Liyue story begins with the death of Rex Lapis, the reigning geo archon and ruler of Liyue. The murder mystery introduces several key characters including Childe and Zhongli. The Fatui are discovered to be behind the plot in a bid to steal the geo gnosis.

Inazuma

An Isolated Archipelago Far East of Teyvat Overcome endless thunderstorms and set foot on the islands of red maple and cherry blossoms. On winding shores and towering cliffs, and in forests and mountains full of secrets, witness the Eternity pursued by Her Excellency, the Almighty Narukami Ogosho.

Sumeru

The city of scholars located in the west-central part of Teyvat. A fantastical nation of both lush rainforest and barren desert, where countless fruits of wisdom grow and are buried. Whether Travelers travel from afar through the forest to reach the academy city or delve deep into the desert to discover the historical ruins of the red desert, a wealth of valuable knowledge awaits them here.

Fontaine

Fontaine is a region in Genshin Impact where Act IV of the main story takes place1. It is presided over by the yet unnamed Hydro Archon and values justice above all else1. The region also seems to prize technology and the arts, and has a newspaper publication read all across Teyvat known as “The Steambird”1. To unlock Fontaine's main story, you need to reach 40 adventure ranks and complete chapter 6 of the 3th volume of the Archons' quests "Caribert"2.
